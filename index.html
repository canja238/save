About Us

At Rice Husk Furniture, we are passionate about sustainability and innovation, transforming rice husk into beautiful, functional, and eco-friendly furniture. Our mission is to revolutionize the furniture industry by harnessing the power of agricultural by-products, reducing waste, and contributing to a greener planet.

Rice husk, a natural by-product of rice milling, is often discarded as waste, contributing to environmental problems. At Rice Husk Furniture, we see opportunity where others see waste. Through cutting-edge technology and eco-conscious design, we convert rice husk into high-quality, durable materials used to craft a wide range of furniture ‚Äì from elegant chairs and tables to modern cabinets, desks, and sofas.

Our furniture pieces are not only stylish and functional but also environmentally friendly. By recycling rice husk, we reduce the need for harmful, non-renewable materials, while also lowering our carbon footprint. The result is furniture that stands the test of time while making a positive impact on the environment.
